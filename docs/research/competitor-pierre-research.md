# Pierre - Competitor Research

**Research Date:** April 13, 2026

---

## Overview

**Important Note on Naming:** There are two distinct apps named "Pierre" (or "Piere") in the personal finance space. This document covers both, with primary focus on **Piere** (piere.com), the more established US-based product, and a secondary section on **Pierre by CloudWalk**, a Brazilian conversational AI finance app.

---

### Primary Subject: Piere (piere.com)

**Piere** is an AI-powered personal finance and money automation platform founded in December 2023 and headquartered in New York, NY. It markets itself as "self-driving money" — a platform that not only tracks finances but actively automates savings, transfers, and debt repayment without requiring the user to be financial experts or open new bank accounts.

- **Founded:** December 2023
- **Headquarters:** New York, NY
- **Founder & CEO:** Yuval Shmul Shuminer (Wharton W19, former BCG consultant)
- **Funding:** $2.1M pre-seed round (October 2025), led by Grand Ventures. Additional investors: Selah Ventures, TruStage Ventures, Samvid Ventures, Fabric VC. Angel investors include Aditya Khurjekar (Money20/20 co-founder), Nick Calamari (Better.com), and Joe Lieberman (K20 Fund).
- **Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web (beta)
- **App Store ID:** id6449256238
- **App Store Rating:** 4.4/5 stars (642 ratings as of early 2026)
- **Media Coverage:** Forbes, CNBC, Fortune, CNET, Business Insider
- **Key Stats:** $1.51B lifetime user growth tracked; 95% bank coverage in US & Canada; users save an average of $6,500/year per platform claims

### Secondary Subject: Pierre by CloudWalk

**Pierre** (io.cloudwalk.pierre) is a Brazilian AI-native personal finance assistant developed by fintech company CloudWalk (parent of InfinitePay). It was founded by Lucas Porto, launched in July 2025, and acquired by CloudWalk in August 2025. It is primarily targeted at Brazilian consumers and is available on Google Play.

- **Founded/Launched:** July 2025
- **Acquired by CloudWalk:** August 2025
- **Headquarters:** São Paulo, Brazil
- **Founder:** Lucas Porto
- **Key Metrics (as of March 2026):** 64M+ transactions processed; 1M+ messages exchanged; 175,000+ active users; 175,000 conversations
- **Announced at:** BusinessWire press release dated March 30, 2026

*The remainder of this document focuses on Piere (US), which is the more relevant competitor for a US-based personal finance product.*

## Product Features & Capabilities

### Core Features

1. **MyPlan™ — Personalized Financial Roadmap**
   - AI-generated, dynamic financial plan tailored to the user's goals, income, and spending habits
   - Automatically adjusts as income and behavior change
   - Creates a goal-driven roadmap for savings, debt payoff, and cash flow

2. **Moves™ — Automated Money Transfers**
   - The flagship "self-driving money" feature, launched December 23, 2025
   - Automatically transfers money between connected accounts based on user-defined or AI-crafted rules
   - Triggers: on payday, when balances hit thresholds, or when spending patterns shift
   - Example: "Move $200 to savings every time I receive a payroll deposit"
   - Debt payoff Moves (avalanche method — highest-interest first) in development for later 2026
   - Platinum tier includes 10 monthly transfers

3. **AI Budgeting**
   - Two-tap automatic budget creation using historical transaction data
   - AI auto-categorizes transactions with ~95% accuracy
   - Custom transaction categories and automated rules
   - Rollovers and budget adjustments in seconds
   - Calendar heatmap view showing high-spending days

4. **Net Worth Tracking**
   - Current net worth dashboard across all connected accounts
   - Historical net worth (paid tier only)
   - Connects to: checking, savings, credit cards, brokerages (Fidelity, Schwab, Betterment, Wealthfront, Vanguard), and crypto (Coinbase)

5. **Spending Analysis & Insights**
   - AI-driven spending pattern detection and alerts
   - "Safe to Spend" limit indicators
   - Cashflow analysis and bill management
   - Reports with unlimited filters (paid tier)
   - Refund and reconciliation detection

6. **Venmo Integration**
   - First-of-its-kind: Piere looks inside Venmo transactions and matches reimbursements to original expenses
   - Treats split payments accurately rather than flagging them as generic income

7. **Peer-to-Peer Payment Splitting & Money Pools**
   - Share payment requests directly from your transactions list
   - Create group pools for events/activities (paid upfront)

8. **Bank Connectivity**
   - Covers 95%+ of US and Canadian financial institutions
   - Uses Plaid integration (added in 2025) for broader institutional coverage
   - SOC 2 Type II audited security

9. **AI Insights**
   - AI identifies spending vulnerabilities and blind spots
   - Subscription detection (helped one reviewer cancel 7 unused subscriptions saving $127/month)
   - Personalized recommendations

### Platforms
- iOS (primary)
- Android
- Web (beta as of late 2025)

## Pricing Tiers

Piere uses a freemium model with three tiers:

| Feature | Piere Purple (Free) | Piere Plus+ | Piere Platinum |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly Price** | $0 | $6.67/mo (billed $79.99/yr) | $10/mo (billed $119.99/yr) |
| **Free Trial** | N/A | 7-day free trial | 7-day free trial |
| **Connected Accounts** | 3 accounts | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Data Providers** | Single | Multiple | Multiple |
| **Budget Creation** | Basic | Automated AI creation | Automated AI creation |
| **Net Worth** | Current only | Historical | Historical |
| **Reporting Filters** | Limited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Transaction Automations** | Basic | Advanced | Advanced |
| **Moves (Auto Transfers)** | No | No | 10 transfers/month |
| **AI Plans (MyPlan™)** | Limited | Limited | Unlimited AI-created plans |
| **Savings AutoMove** | No | No | Yes (on payday) |
| **Money-Back Guarantee** | No | No | $120 guarantee |

**Notes on Pricing:**
- The $9.99/month price is also cited in some sources, possibly reflecting a legacy monthly (non-annual) price for Plus+
- Platinum at $119.99/year includes a $120 money-back guarantee — effectively a risk-free annual subscription
- Reviewers have noted some confusion around subscription levels and pricing changes

## Target Market & Positioning

### Primary Target Audience
- **Former Mint users** (Mint shut down in January 2024; Piere was explicitly built as a Mint alternative)
- **Millennials and Gen Z** who are tech-savvy but overwhelmed by manual budgeting
- **Frequent Venmo users** who want split payments tracked accurately
- **People with debt** (credit cards, auto loans) who want automated payoff strategies
- **Passive wealth-builders** who want automation over active financial management
- **US and Canadian users** (95%+ bank coverage across both countries)

### Positioning Statement
"Self-driving money" — Piere positions itself not just as a tracker but as an executor. The central message is: *you set the goal, Piere moves the money.* This contrasts with passive budgeting tools (YNAB, Monarch Money) that require ongoing manual input.

### Competitive Differentiation Claims
- **vs. Copilot Money:** Free tier available; Copilot charges ~$95/year with no free tier. Piere claims SOC 2 Type II certification; Copilot reportedly does not.
- **vs. Mint:** Piere was purpose-built to replace Mint, offering migration tools, similar free model, and superior automation.
- **vs. YNAB:** Less friction and manual entry; automated vs. manual zero-based budgeting philosophy.
- **Unique:** Venmo integration for split payment reconciliation; actual money movement (not just tracking).

### Brand Voice
Community-focused, approachable, and responsive. The team engages users directly on Reddit daily. Founder Yuval Shuminer has positioned the company around democratizing personal finance for people who previously "shied away from financial management."

### App Store (iOS)
- **Rating:** 4.4 / 5 stars
- **Review Count:** 642 ratings (as of early 2026)

**Positive quotes:**
- *"It's free, simple, syncs bank transactions, has an aesthetic look, and allows for unlimited customizable budget categories."*

- Commonly cited as a strong Mint alternative
- Key concern raised: budget feature doesn't enforce balanced budgets — one user's recommended budget showed a $4,100/month deficit
- Account sync disconnections (especially with Chase) reported by multiple users

### Product Hunt
- **Upvotes:** 67 points (Daily Rank: #42 on launch day, September 11, 2023)
- **Followers:** 24
- **Reviews:** 0 formal written reviews on Product Hunt

### Bankerology 90-Day Real-World Test
One reviewer ran a rigorous 90-day test with documented results:
- Savings grew from $1,200 → $3,800 (+$2,600)
- Paid off one credit card ($1,200 balance)
- Identified $3,200 in "found money" through subscription cuts
- DoorDash spending dropped from $340 → $120/month after AI flagged the pattern
- Canceled 7 unused subscriptions saving $127/month
- Verdict: *"For me, absolutely"* worth the subscription cost

### General Sentiment Summary
Piere has strong positive sentiment among engaged users who see real financial results. Criticism clusters around: (1) inconsistent customer support response times, (2) Android app quality lagging behind iOS, (3) occasional account sync failures, and (4) confusing subscription tier communication.

## Key Strengths

1. **True Automation:** Moves™ feature actually transfers money — not just tracks it — making it unique among most personal finance apps.
2. **Accessible Free Tier:** Piere Purple is free with meaningful features, capturing the large post-Mint user pool.
3. **Strong AI Categorization:** ~95% transaction categorization accuracy reported by multiple reviewers.
4. **Venmo Integration:** Unique capability that accurately handles split payments and reimbursements.
5. **Community Engagement:** Founder and team actively participate in Reddit and user forums, creating strong brand loyalty.
6. **Proven ROI:** Real-world reviews show measurable savings outcomes (avg. $6,500/year per platform claims; individual case studies confirm $2,600+ saved in 90 days).
7. **SOC 2 Type II Certified:** Strong security posture for a startup.
8. **Momentum:** 3x user growth, 4x paid subscriber growth in 2025; $2.1M funding secured.
9. **Female-Founded Fintech:** Yuval Shuminer's profile has driven notable press coverage (Forbes, CNBC, Fortune, CNET, Business Insider).
10. **Roadmap Responsiveness:** Delivered Plaid integration after user requests; roadmap is publicly available and voted on by users.

---

## Key Weaknesses

1. **Android App Quality:** Multiple users report the Android app feels "half-baked" with broken notifications and poor UX compared to iOS.
2. **Customer Support:** Support response times are inconsistent; some users waited 5+ days without any acknowledgment.
3. **Budget Accuracy:** The budget tool does not enforce balanced budgets — users have been shown monthly deficit plans without clear warnings.
4. **Account Sync Reliability:** Recurring issues with bank disconnections (especially Chase), even after Plaid was added.
5. **Very Small Trustpilot Presence:** Only 5 reviews on Trustpilot with a 3.4/5 average — low sample credibility but the negative reviews raise real concerns.
6. **Moves Limited on Platinum:** Only 10 transfers/month on the highest tier, which may feel restrictive for power users.
7. **Subscription Tier Confusion:** Users report unclear communication about what's included in each tier and when pricing changes.
8. **Privacy Concerns:** The language in Piere's terms around the right to transfer funds (a technical necessity for Moves) has alarmed some users who interpret it as unexpected.
9. **No Full Desktop App:** Web version is still in beta; primarily a mobile product.
10. **Small Funding Base:** $2.1M pre-seed is relatively modest for a fintech with ambitions to automate money movement; longevity and feature development pace may be constrained.

## Secondary App: Pierre by CloudWalk (Brazil)

For completeness, here is a brief profile of the other "Pierre" personal finance app:

- **Company:** CloudWalk (parent of InfinitePay), São Paulo, Brazil
- **Founder:** Lucas Porto
- **Launched:** July 2025; acquired by CloudWalk August 2025
- **Market:** Brazilian consumers
- **Platform:** Android (Google Play: io.cloudwalk.pierre)
- **Core Concept:** Conversational AI finance assistant — no charts, no spreadsheets, just natural language. Three AI "agents": Albert (daily monitoring/alerts), Marie (bi-weekly behavioral analysis), and Galileo (monthly overview and projections).
- **Key Metrics (March 2026):** 64M+ transactions processed, 1M+ messages, 175,000 conversations, 175,000+ active users
- **Pricing:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Press:** BusinessWire press release published March 30, 2026: "Pierre Turns Financial Management Into a Simple Conversation — No Spreadsheets, No Confusing Charts"
- **Relevance to US Market:** Currently Brazil-focused; the March 2026 announcement signals potential US expansion ambitions (press was published via BusinessWire in English and CloudWalk has a US entity, JIM.com).
